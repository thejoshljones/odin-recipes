Gilled Cheese

Home

Grilled cheese is an easy, low ingredient recipe.

Ingredients

  • White sliced bread
  • Butter
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  2. Generously apply butter to one side of sliced bread.
  3. When pan is heated, put slice of bread butter side down on cooking surface.
  4. Put sliced cheese on other side of bread.
  5. Place another slice of buttered bread on top of the cheese, butter side up.
  6. After the bread that is cooking gets golden brown, flip to the other side of the sandwich.
  7. Cut the sandwich from one corner to the other into two triangles.
  8. Enjoy
