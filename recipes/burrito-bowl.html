Burrito Bowl

Home

A burito bowl is one of my favorite home made meals.

Ingredients

  • Brown rice
  • Black beans
  • Bell pepper
  • Poblaño
  • Onion
  • Avocado
  • Queso fresco
  • Pepita
  • Hot sauce
  • Lime juice

Steps

  1. Cook brown rice.
  2. Slice pepper, poblaño and onion into strips.
  3. Cook pepper, poblaño and onion. Season to taste.
  4. Heat black beans.
  5. Dice avocado into 1/4 inch pieces.
  6. Break queso fresco into chunks.
  7. When rice is finished, season with lime juice
  8. Top with pepitas and hot sauce
